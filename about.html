About Us

Sangum Tyres, an exclusive MRF dealership in Jamshedpur, has been serving customers with excellence for nearly a decade. Founded by Imdadullah Habibur Rahman, an experienced civil engineer who transitioned into the tyre business at the age of 45, Sangum Tyres has become one of the most trusted names in the region. Under his leadership, the dealership has built a strong reputation for offering a wide range of high-quality tyres, catering to everything from motorcycles to heavy-duty vehicles.
Sangum Tyres is committed to providing top-notch products and exceptional customer service. Whether you're looking for tyres for your personal vehicle or need specialised solutions for commercial and industrial vehicles, Sangum Tyres has the expertise and inventory to meet your needs. With years of experience and a customer-first approach, Sangum Tyres continues to be the go-to tyre provider for many in Jamshedpur.
